Why Thrift Fashion?

Buying thrift clothing is a sustainable fashion choice. By shopping thrift clothing as an alternative to buying new clothing, we reduce the demand for new items and the environmental impact they create. By keeping thrift clothing items in circulation we power the circular fashion economy, whilst lowering the number of fashion items sent to landfill.
